# User Stories

## AE-1: User Registration

As a new visitor, I want to create an account by providing my name and email, so that I can manage my orders and save my profile details.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Navigate to 'Signup / Login' page.
2. Enter name and unique email.
3. Complete full registration form.
4. Verify 'ACCOUNT CREATED!' message is visible.

---

## AE-2: Login with Valid Credentials

As a registered user, I want to log in using my email and password, so that I can access my account features.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Enter valid email and password.
2. Click 'Login'.
3. Verify 'Logged in as [username]' is visible.

---

## AE-3: Logout Functionality

As a logged-in user, I want to log out of my account, so that I can secure my session on shared devices.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Click 'Logout' button.
2. Verify redirection to the login page.

---

## AE-4: Search Product

As a shopper, I want to search for products using keywords, so that I can quickly find specific items I want to buy.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Enter product name in search bar.
2. Click search icon.
3. Verify all results contain the search term.

---

## AE-5: Add Products to Cart

As a shopper, I want to add items to the shopping cart, so that I can group them for a single purchase.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Hover over a product and click 'Add to Cart'.
2. Verify 'Added!' modal appears.
3. Verify product exists in the 'Cart' page.

---

## AE-6: Newsletter Subscription

As a visitor, I want to subscribe to the newsletter in the footer, so that I can receive updates on new products and deals.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Scroll to footer.
2. Enter email in subscription field.
3. Verify 'You have been successfully subscribed!' message.

---

## AE-7: Contact Us Form

As a user, I want to submit a contact form with a file attachment, so that I can get support or provide feedback.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Fill in name, email, and message.
2. Upload a file.
3. Click 'Submit' and confirm browser alert.
4. Verify success message.

---

## AE-8: View Product Detail

As a shopper, I want to view the details of a specific product, so that I can see the price and description before buying.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Click 'View Product' on any item.
2. Verify Product Name, Category, Price, and Condition are visible.

---

## AE-9: Modify Cart Quantity

As a shopper, I want to increase the quantity of a product in the cart, so that I can purchase multiple units of the same item.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Navigate to Product Detail.
2. Set quantity to 4.
3. Add to cart.
4. Verify quantity in cart is exactly 4.

---

## AE-10: Remove Product from Cart

As a shopper, I want to remove items from my cart, so that I can change my mind before checking out.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Navigate to 'Cart'.
2. Click the 'X' button next to a product.
3. Verify the product is removed.

---

## AE-11: Checkout with Registration

As a shopper, I want to register an account during the checkout process, so that I can complete my order as a new customer.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Add items to cart.
2. Click 'Proceed to Checkout'.
3. Click 'Register / Login'.
4. Complete registration and return to cart.

---

## AE-12: Verify Address in Checkout

As a user, I want to see my delivery and billing addresses during checkout, so that I can ensure my items are sent to the correct location.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Proceed to checkout while logged in.
2. Verify Delivery and Billing Addresses match registration data.

---

## AE-13: Place Order & Payment

As a logged-in user, I want to enter payment details and place an order, so that I can finalize my purchase.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Enter Card details (Name, Number, CVC, Expiry).
2. Click 'Pay and Confirm Order'.
3. Verify 'ORDER PLACED!' message.

---

## AE-14: Download Invoice

As a customer, I want to download my invoice after a successful order, so that I can keep a record of my purchase.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Complete an order.
2. Click 'Download Invoice' button on success page.
3. Verify file download initiates.

---

## AE-15: Filter by Category

As a shopper, I want to browse products by specific categories, so that I can find items relevant to my needs.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Click on a Category (e.g., Women).
2. Click a Sub-category (e.g., Dress).
3. Verify only relevant products appear.

---

## AE-16: Filter by Brand

As a shopper, I want to filter products by brand name, so that I can find products from my favorite manufacturers.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Click a brand name in sidebar (e.g., Polo).
2. Verify page title displays brand name.
3. Verify items match selected brand.

---

## AE-17: Add Product Review

As a customer, I want to write a review on a product page, so that I can share my experience with other shoppers.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Go to 'View Product'.
2. Fill Name, Email, and Review text.
3. Click 'Submit'.
4. Verify 'Thank you for your review' message.

---

## AE-18: Add Recommended Items

As a shopper, I want to add products from the 'Recommended Items' section, so that I can quickly buy popular suggested products.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Scroll to the bottom of the Home page.
2. Click 'Add to Cart' on a recommended item.
3. Verify item is in the cart.

---

## AE-19: Verify Test Cases Page

As a QA student, I want to access the 'Test Cases' page, so that I can see the manual scenarios for automation practice.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Click 'Test Cases' button.
2. Verify redirection to the test cases URL.

---

## AE-20: Delete Account

As a user, I want to delete my account, so that I can remove my personal data from the site.

Acceptance Criteria:
1. Log in to an account.
2. Click 'Delete Account' button.
3. Verify 'ACCOUNT DELETED!' message is visible.

---
